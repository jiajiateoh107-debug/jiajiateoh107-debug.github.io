What it does

Simple Inventory Planner is a Google Sheets add-on that helps e-commerce sellers and inventory managers calculate reorder quantities, set safety stock levels, and receive automated email alerts before stockouts happen.

Features

Replenishment Planning

Calculate how much stock to reorder based on sales velocity and lead time.

Safety Stock Alerts

Get email notifications when inventory drops below your defined thresholds.

Scheduled Reports

Set up daily or weekly automated checks so you never miss a low-stock situation.

Works in Google Sheets

No new tools to learn — works inside the spreadsheet you already use every day.

Permissions used

  • Google Sheets (current only) — read and write data in the active spreadsheet
  • Script Storage — save your settings and preferences
  • UI — display the sidebar panel inside Sheets
  • Email (Send Mail) — send you low-stock alert emails
  • Triggers (ScriptApp) — schedule automated checks on your behalf
